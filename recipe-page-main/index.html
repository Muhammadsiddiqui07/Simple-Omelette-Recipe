Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. This classic Omelette combines beaten eggs
cooked to perfection , optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, or meats

Preparation Time

  • Total:Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation:5 minutes
  • Cooking:5 minutes

Ingredient

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Paper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional filling: cheese , diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs

Instruction

1. Beat the eggs: In a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch od salt and paper until they are well mixed. You can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fulffier texture

2. Heat the pan: Place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter and oil

3. Cook the Omelette: Once the butter is melted and bubbling, pour in the eggs. till the pan to ensure the eggs evenly coat the surface

4. Add filling (optional): when the eggs begain to set the edges but are still slightly runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over one half of the Omelette

5. Fold and serve: As the Omelette continous to cook, carefully lift one edge and fold it over the filling. Let it cook for another minute, then slide it onto the plate

6. Enjoy Serve hot, with additional salt and papper if nedded
